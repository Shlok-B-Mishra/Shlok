About Me

Starting a career in design on your own is a tough task, especially when you don’t have any educational background in this field. A source of motivation is constantly required to bypass the ups and downs of this journey. For a moment, a feeling to back out may hit the head and it’s the time when you’ve just started getting the taste of what design actually is.

I always wanted to share my experiences about how my journey started but never had a chance nor did I feel myself to be capable enough to do it. However, now I think that if my story can motivate someone, it’s worth sharing.
